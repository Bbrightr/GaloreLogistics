Company Profile

Galore Logistics was established in 2009, as an enterprise known as BR Trading Enterprise that was in the business of procuring agro commodities like raw cashew nut, sesame, maize and shear nut among other commodities. BR Trading rose to become a 3PL Company. The company moved from an enterprise to limited liability Company which was known as BR Impex Limited in 2012 and its name was changed to GALORE INVESTMENT LIMITED.

With the prospective vision and ambition to become a one-stop institution in international trade and freight forwarding for all parties we conduct business with, considerable effort was made in investing in the right branding human resource to position us as a formidable presence in international trade and supply chain and thus our new name GALORE LOGISTICS!

The company has over the years transacted business with many local and foreign companies and has built its reputation on multiple grounded principles.
