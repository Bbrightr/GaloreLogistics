Company Profile

Galore Investment Limited was established in 2009, as an enterprise known as BR Trading Enterprise that was in the business of procuring agro commodities like raw cashew nut, sesame, maize and shear nut among other commodities. BR Trading rose to become a 3PL Company. The company move from an enterprise to limited liability Company which was known as BR Impex Limited in 2012 and its name was change to GALORE INVESTMENT LIMITED.

With the prospective vision and ambition to become a one-stop institution in international trade and freight forwarding for all parties we conduct business with, considerable effort was made in investing in the right branding human resource to position us as a formidable presence in international trade and supply chain an thus our new name GALORE LOGISTICS!

The company has over the years transacted business with many local and foreign companies and has built its reputation on the following principle:

  • Integrity
  • Efficiency in the field of work
  • Promptness of response
  • Variety and level of services offered
  • Consistency
  • Hassle free service
  • Cost effective service

Our staffs are experienced and well trained by Ghana Institute of Freight Forwarders (GIFF), specialize in dealing with various goods

With the company rising-to-the-top, ambition has really given us much experience in the supply chain management approach to international trade.
Yet the sky is the limit, so we need you to grow with us and so at GALORE LOGISTICS "Together We Grow".
