What is Freight Forwarding?

We arrange the movement of your cargo by sea, air, or land from one location to another. Galore Logistics Limited works with trusted global partners to ensure your goods arrive safely, on time, and at competitive rates.

Whether you're shipping internationally or domestically, our freight forwarding services provide end-to-end solutions that simplify complex logistics operations. We handle documentation, customs clearance, tracking, and delivery—so you can focus on your business.

✈️ Multi-Modal Solutions

Sea freight, air cargo, or land transport. We optimize routes for cost-efficiency and speed.

Carnet Services

A Carnet is an international customs document that provides duty-free customs clearance for temporary imports into foreign countries. It simplifies and expedites customs entry, reducing delays and costs.

Benefits:

  • Duty-free temporary imports
  • Simplified customs procedures
  • Accepted in 70+ countries
  • Reduces documentation burden
